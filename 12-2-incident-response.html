Strategy Action Use When

  • Don't reboot: Lose volatile memory evidence

ğŸ’¡ Interview Tip

Question: "Walk me through how you'd respond to a ransomware incident."

Answer: "First, immediately isolate affected systems - network quarantine via EDR or unplug from network. Don't turn them off to preserve memory. Second, identify the scope - which systems are encrypted, what's the ransomware strain. Third, assess backup status - can we restore? Fourth, involve stakeholders - legal, management, potentially law enforcement. Fifth, make a recovery decision - restore from backups is preferred. Sixth, rebuild affected systems from known-good images. Finally, conduct post-incident review - how did they get in, what do we need to fix. We never recommend paying the ransom."
